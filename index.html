Columbia Women and Gender Diverse Ultimate

ABOUT

Columbia Women and Gender Diverse Ultimate is a D-1 club sport that welcomes students with all levels of Ultimate experience from any of Columbia's undergraduate and graduate schools. We seek to foster spirited play and build team chemistry on and off the fields. To accommodate students seeking varying levels of commitment and competition, we have an A team (Pandemic) as well as a B team (BaeWatch). Both teams compete against other colleges and universities along the East Coast.

Schedule

Both teams practice Tuesdays and Thursdays from 7:30-10:30 at Baker Athletic Complex. Pandemic has an additional practice on Sundays from 6:30-9:30. Stay tuned for the Fall 2020 schedule!

Donate

In recent years, our program has grown and now includes nearly 90 student athletes between our two teams. This year, club sports at Columbia received a decrease in funding allocations. This, along with the immense growth of our program, means that we need extra help to fund tournament travel, accommodations, and bid fees for this spring season. We want to make this team and our tournaments as accessible as possible to every single one of our teammates and need your help! We have two ways to donate: our GoFundMe, or through the Columbia website.
