About

Columbia Womenâ€™s Ultimate is a D-1 club sport that welcomes students with all levels of Ultimate experience from any of Columbia's undergraduate and graduate schools. We seek to foster spirited play and build team chemistry on and off the fields. To accommodate students seeking varying levels of commitment and competition, we have an A team (Pandemic) as well as a B team (BaeWatch). Both teams compete against other colleges and universities along the East Coast. For the 2016-17 season, Pandemic finished 3rd in the Metro East region and BaeWatch tied for 9th at its second Sectional appearance.
