Columbia Women's Ultimate

About

Columbia Women’s Ultimate is a D-1 club sport that welcomes students with all levels of Ultimate experience from any of Columbia's undergraduate and graduate schools. We seek to foster spirited play and build team chemistry on and off the fields. To accommodate students seeking varying levels of commitment and competition, we have an A team (Pandemic) as well as a B team (BaeWatch). Both teams compete against other colleges and universities along the East Coast. For the 2016-17 season, Pandemic finished 3rd in the Metro East region and BaeWatch tied for 9th at its second Sectional appearance.

Schedule

Both teams practice Tuesdays and Thursdays from 7:30-10:30 at Baker Athletic Complex. Pandemic has an additional practice on Sundays from 6:30-9:30.

Donate

In recent years, our program has grown and now includes nearly 90 female student athletes between our two teams. This year, club sports at Columbia received a decrease in funding allocations. This, along with the immense growth of our program, means that we need extra help to fund tournament travel, accommodations, and bid fees for this spring season. We want to make this team and our tournaments as accessible as possible to every single one of our teammates and need your help!
We're currently running a GoFundMe campaign and we're aiming to reach $3500 by March 1st!

In addition to our GoFundMe, we also continually accept tax-deductable donations-- follow the instructions below!

1. Go to this URL: https://giving.columbia.edu/giveonline/

2. Under School or Division: select "Athletics"

3. Under Designation: select "Other" 4. Enter a gift amount then click NEXT 5. Under Comments/Special Instructions: Type "Columbia University Women's Ultimate Club gift"

Thanks for your support! If you have any questions about our program, don't hesitate to contact us!
